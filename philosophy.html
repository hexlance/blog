General Philosophy Resources


Learning Aids


v1 Philosophy Reading Sheet by Hexlance

An open Google spreadsheet of mine that contains basic primary philosophical titles in an organized sheet so that you can check your reading progress. Unfinished due to incomplete knowledge of philosophy, but it contains most fundamental texts.


Desolhar Philosophy

A highly valuable website that organizes Classical books into topics and has wonderful "mind maps" to accompany each sector/topic.



Primary Sources


MIT The Internet Classics Archive

A free database with 441 works of top-tier classical literature, mostly of Greek or Roman origin. Extremely simple to use. Only possible downside is the old-fashioned text and web design, but honestly, I think it's more charming that way.


Scaife Viewer (by the Perseus Digital Library)

A larger and much more polished database that holds over 2,285 works with translations. A bit more sophisticated than Classics Archive with a powerful search tool as well.



Encyclopedias & Articles


Internet Encyclopedia of Philosophy (IEP)

Founded in 1995, this is the definitive (and free) philosophy encyclopedia with detailed, peer-edited articles on all areas of philosophy.


Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy (SEP)

Another popular dynamic philosophy encyclopedia that may be easier to dive right into.
